Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Edit Delete

Indulge in the creamy, savory delight of Classic Spaghetti Carbonara. This traditional Italian dish combines al dente spaghetti with crispy pancetta, rich egg yolks, and a generous amount of Parmesan cheese. The result is a velvety, flavorful sauce that coats each strand of pasta perfectly. A sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper adds the finishing touch to this simple yet decadent meal.
